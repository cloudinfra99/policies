Refund Policy

At Cloudinfra, we want you to be satisfied with our service. If you are not happy with your purchase, we are here to help.

1. Refund Eligibility

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. If you are unhappy with the service, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase.

2. Exceptions

Refunds are not available for:

  • Accounts that have violated our Terms of Service.
  • Usage-based charges already consumed.
  • Requests made after the 14-day window.

3. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, please email contact@cloudinfra.tech with your order number and the reason for your request. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method via Lemon Squeezy within 5-10 business days.

4. Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time. You will continue to have access to the service until the end of your current billing period.
